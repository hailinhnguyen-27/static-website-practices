Where Imagination Comes to Play!

Step into a world of wonder, laughter, and endless creativity. At ToyWonder, we believe that every child deserves a magical childhood filled with joy, curiosity, and fun! From brain-boosting puzzles to giggle-inducing plushies, our toys are designed to spark smiles and fuel imagination.

At ToyWonder, we’re more than just a toy store – we’re a place where imagination begins and childhood memories are made. Founded with love and a deep passion for early childhood development, our mission is to bring high-quality, safe, and joy-filled toys to families across Vietnam.


From babies taking their first steps to curious kids building their dream worlds, we offer a handpicked selection of toys that spark creativity, encourage learning, and support every stage of a child’s growth.


We believe that play is powerful – it shapes thinking, builds bonds, and lights up little eyes with wonder. That’s why every toy on our shelves is chosen with care, tested for safety, and packed with fun!

The Adventure of Little Hana and Her Toy Kingdom


One sunny afternoon, little Hana opened her toy box and pulled out her favorite plush dragon, whom she named Zuzu. As soon as she gave Zuzu a warm hug, poof! — the living room faded away, and Hana found herself in a magical toy kingdom.

The blocks she'd stacked earlier had become towering castles. Her tiny cars raced through rainbow roads. Stuffed animals bowed to her as the Queen of Playland. With Zuzu by her side, Hana helped lost teddy bears find their way home, shared cookies at a tea party with her dolls, and flew over cotton-candy clouds on a paper plane.

When Mommy called her for dinner, the kingdom gently disappeared. Hana looked at Zuzu and whispered, “Let’s go back tomorrow.”
